WARHAMMER 40.000

Introduction

It is the 41st millenium. Humanity is constantly on the brink of extinction due to various threats: the vile, daemonic powers of Chaos, the alien and strange forces of Xenos and humanity itself. For in the grim darkness of the far future, there is only war.

Welcome to Warhammer 40.000, also known as Warhammer 40K! This is a very vast and expansive universe that I and many other partake in. In Warhammer 40K, there is only death and destruction. Every faction, such as the Imperium of Man, the Aeldari, the Orks, the Necrons, the forces of Chaos, the Tyranids, is some flavor of evil. Something about them makes them evil. The Imperium of Man is a highly fanatical, xenophobic and extermely religious group of people that worship their god, the God-Emperor of Mankind. The Aeldari are essentially space elves, complete with magical powers. Chaos is the main "evil" and reside in the Warp, being certain aspects of people amplified to the max, such as Khorne being about honorable combat but also blood and murder. Orks, they just want a good old time fighting with people and are just as likely to kill each other as they are to kill a human or an Eldar (one of those space elves). Tyranids are giant bugs looking for their next meal. The T'au fight for the "greater good", while also essentially brainwashing whoever joins them. If any (or all!) of that sounds interesting, feel free to take a look around! If not, thank you for even reading this far!
